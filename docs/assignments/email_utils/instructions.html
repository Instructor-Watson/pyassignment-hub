Goal

Implement two functions:

  1. is_valid_email(s) → True/False for simple emails (letters/digits, dots and underscores before @; a domain with at least one dot).
  2. extract_user_domain(s) → returns (user, domain) if valid, else None.

Guidance

  • Keep the regex readable with raw strings (r"...").
  • Don't aim for RFC-perfect validation—keep it simple and explain limitations in a comment.
